Who We Are

Where Academic Rigour
Meets Industry Practice

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments. We exist at the intersection of academic insight and operational reality — equipping practitioners with skills that are immediately applicable in the field.

Through our UK university partnership, IDR combines cutting-edge research with real-world projects, ensuring every programme reflects the evolving threat landscape. Whether you are an organisation seeking certified talent or a professional seeking to advance your career, IDR provides the structured pathway from learning to deployment.

What We Do

Three Pillars.
One Mission.

IDR operates across three integrated service pillars — each designed to advance digital risk capability at individual, organisational, and systemic levels.

01 / 03

IDR Academy

Structured bootcamps and professional development programmes for students, career changers, and practitioners. Our curriculum bridges academic foundations with hands-on, scenario-based risk training.

Innovation & Incubation

Advisory Services
